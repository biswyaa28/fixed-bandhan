# Bandhan AI — Pitch Deck Speaker Notes

> Read these BEFORE presenting. Don't read them during the pitch.

---

## Slide 1: Cover (15 seconds)

"Hi, I'm [Name], co-founder of Bandhan AI. We're building India's first AI-powered marriage matchmaking app — with government-verified profiles and tools for family involvement. We're raising ₹25 lakhs pre-seed."

**Tip:** Make eye contact. Smile. This sets the tone for everything.

---

## Slide 2: The Problem (90 seconds)

"180 million single Indians between 20-35 are looking for a life partner. But they're stuck between two terrible options.

Option 1: Dating apps like Tinder and Bumble. These are designed for casual dating, not marriage. 78% of Indian users on these apps actually want marriage — but the apps aren't built for that. And 73% quit because of fake profiles.

Option 2: Legacy sites like Shaadi.com. These cost ₹5,000 to ₹25,000 a year. The interfaces look like they were built in 2010. And younger users — people under 30 — find them completely irrelevant.

Here's a quote from one of our beta users: 'I want to get married, but Tinder is for hookups and Shaadi feels like my parents' generation.'

There's a massive gap in the market. And that's exactly where Bandhan sits."

**If asked:** "Where did you get these stats?" — RedSeer Consulting 2025 report + Statista dating app survey.

---

## Slide 3: Our Solution (60 seconds)

"Bandhan AI sits at the intersection of modern dating UX and Indian marriage values.

Five key differentiators:

First — our AI scores compatibility on values, intent, and lifestyle. Not just photos.

Second — every profile is verified through DigiLocker. That's India's official government identity system. Three levels: phone, Aadhaar, photo match. Zero fakes.

Third — Family View PDF. It's a shareable, printable summary of a match. Designed for parents. Because in India, marriage is a family decision — and no app acknowledges that.

Fourth — safety is built into the core. A 'Share My Date' button, women-message-first option, 24-hour moderation.

And fifth — we're 10 times cheaper than Shaadi.com. ₹499 a month, with a 7-day free trial."

## Slide 4: Market (45 seconds)

"The Indian online dating market will be $1.2 billion by 2027, growing 15% year over year.

78% of that market is marriage-seekers — that's our $936 million addressable segment.

Bottom-up: 40 million Indians are on dating or matrimony apps. If we capture just 1% — 310,000 users — with 4% paid conversion at ₹400 ARPU, that's ₹1.5 crore ARR.

The market is massive, growing, and underserved."

## Common Tough Questions

### "This is a crowded market"
"It's a big market with bad products. 73% abandonment proves incumbents haven't solved the problem. We're not competing with Tinder — we're replacing Shaadi.com for the next generation."

### "Why will this work in India specifically?"
"Because we've built for Indian culture from day one. Family View PDF, vegetarian filters, joint-family preferences, DigiLocker verification — these aren't features you can bolt onto a Western app."

### "What if someone copies this?"
"Our moat is the combination: DigiLocker verification + AI matching + family tools + comic book brand. Any one feature is copyable. The full system together — built with cultural understanding — is very hard to replicate."

### "₹25 lakhs seems too small"
"It's intentionally small. We want to prove PMF before raising more. A small pre-seed at a low valuation means less dilution for everyone — and we've already built the product with personal funds."
