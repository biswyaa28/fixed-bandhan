## ESOP (Employee Stock Option Pool)

### Pool Size
- **Initial:** 10% of founding equity (1,000 shares)
- **Expanded at seed:** +200 shares (total 1,200 / 10.7%)
- **Expanded at Series A:** +300 shares (total 1,500 / 11.4%)

### Vesting Schedule
- **Cliff:** 1 year (0% vests before 12 months)
- **Vesting period:** 4 years total
- **Schedule:** Monthly vesting after cliff (1/48th per month)
- **Acceleration:** Single-trigger on acquisition for key employees

## Key Terms & Protections

### For Founders
- No excessive dilution in early rounds (<15% per round)
- Founder-friendly board (2 founder seats, 1 investor seat until Series A)
- No full-ratchet anti-dilution (weighted average only)
- No liquidation preference >1x (participating preferred capped at 3x)

### For Investors
- Pro-rata rights in future rounds
- Information rights (quarterly updates)
- Board observer seat (seed round onward)
- Standard anti-dilution protection (weighted average)
- 1x non-participating liquidation preference

- **Entity:** Private Limited Company (India)
