## Key Risks to Projections

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| Slower user growth | Revenue 40-60% lower | Focus on city-by-city density, not pan-India |
| Lower conversion rate | Revenue 30-50% lower | A/B test pricing, improve premium value |
| Higher churn (>10%) | LTV drops 20%+ | Improve matching quality, add retention features |
| Competition from incumbents | Growth slows | Build moat with verification + family features |
| Regulatory changes | Compliance costs rise | DPDP compliance built in from day 1 |
