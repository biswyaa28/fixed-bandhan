# Bandhan AI — Social Media Content Templates

> **Version:** 2026-02-28-v1
> **Platforms:** Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Reddit, WhatsApp
> **Tone:** Warm, genuine, culturally sensitive. Never salesy or desperate.

---

## Brand Voice Guidelines

### DO
- Sound like a trusted friend giving advice
- Use a mix of English and Hindi (Hinglish is fine for social)
- Reference Indian culture, festivals, food, family naturally
- Share real value (tips, insights, stories)
- Be respectful of all communities, religions, and castes

### DON'T
- Use clickbait or fear-based marketing ("You'll die alone!")
- Make fun of arranged marriages or love marriages
- Use explicit or suggestive content
- Share user data or real profiles without consent
- Spam hashtags (max 10 per post)
