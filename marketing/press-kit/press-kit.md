## What Makes Bandhan Different

### 1. AI Matching Beyond Photos
Our algorithm weighs **intent alignment (35%)**, **shared values (25%)**, **lifestyle compatibility (20%)**, **location proximity (12%)**, and **family fit (8%)**. This produces matches that are 3× more likely to lead to real-world meetings compared to photo-only swiping.

### 2. DigiLocker Verification
Every profile is verified through India's **DigiLocker** system (government ID). Users earn Bronze, Silver, or Gold verification badges. This eliminates fake profiles — a problem that plagues 30-40% of profiles on competing platforms.

### 3. Family View PDF
A unique feature that generates a **shareable PDF** with match details in a format Indian parents are comfortable with — education, career, family structure, values. This bridges the gap between modern dating and traditional family approval.

### 4. Safety-First Design
- **"Share My Date"** button for live location sharing with trusted contacts
- **Women-first messaging** mode (optional Bumble-style protection)
- **24-hour moderation** on all reported content
- **Content filtering** for harassment and explicit material

### 5. Privacy by Design
Fully compliant with India's **DPDP Act 2023**. All data stored in India. Photos blurred until matched. Users can export or delete all data at any time.

### 6. Affordable for India
Premium starts at **₹499/month** (~₹17/day) — significantly cheaper than traditional matrimony sites (₹1,000-₹5,000/month). Includes a **7-day free trial**.
