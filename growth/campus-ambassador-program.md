## 1. Program Overview

### What Is It?

The Bandhan AI Campus Ambassador Program recruits college students to organically promote Bandhan in their campus communities. Ambassadors are the face of Bandhan on campus — they're not salespeople, they're trusted friends recommending a product they genuinely use.

### Why Campus?

- **Target demographic**: 22-28 year-olds in India are prime marriage-age users
- **Network density**: College students have large, tightly-connected social networks
- **Word of mouth**: 60% of app installs in India come from peer recommendations
- **Cultural moment**: Final-year students and recent graduates actively think about marriage
- **Cost**: ₹0 cash spend — rewards are Premium days and swag

### Goals (Year 1)

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Colleges covered | 50 |
| Total ambassadors | 200 (4 per college) |
| Signups from campus channel | 5,710 (10% of total) |
| Cost per signup | ₹9 (swag + events only) |
| Ambassador retention (6 months) | 60% |

## 2. Ambassador Profile

### Ideal Candidate

- **Age**: 20-26 (final year undergrad or postgrad)
- **Active social media**: 500+ followers on Instagram or active in college WhatsApp groups
- **Marriage-positive**: Believes in meaningful relationships (not cynical about marriage)
- **Trusted in peer group**: Known as someone who gives good advice
- **Tech-comfortable**: Can explain an app to friends who may not be tech-savvy
- **Bilingual**: Comfortable in both English and Hindi (or regional language)

### NOT Looking For

- ❌ People who just want free stuff
- ❌ Aggressive marketers or MLM-style promoters
- ❌ Anyone who would create fake profiles or use bots
- ❌ Anyone uncomfortable with the marriage/relationship topic

## 3. Recruitment Process

### Phase 1: Outreach (Week 1-2)

**Channels:**
1. **Instagram DMs** to college club pages (Cultural Committee, Student Council)
2. **LinkedIn posts** targeting college students
3. **Reddit** — posts in r/indian_academia, college-specific subreddits
4. **WhatsApp** — reach out to known college group admins
5. **Existing users** — prompt active users to apply

**Application Form (Google Form):**
```
1. Full name
2. College name + year
3. City
4. Instagram handle (or primary social media)
5. Why do you want to be a Bandhan ambassador? (2-3 lines)
6. How many people in your network are thinking about marriage? (rough estimate)
7. What's your idea for promoting Bandhan on your campus?
8. Phone number (for WhatsApp communication)
```

### Phase 2: Selection (Week 3)

**Criteria (scored 1-5 each):**
| Criteria | Weight |
|----------|--------|
| Social reach (followers + group memberships) | 25% |
| Quality of "promotion idea" | 30% |
| College prestige / network size | 20% |
| Communication quality | 25% |

**Accept:** Top 4 applicants per college
**Waitlist:** Next 2 per college

### Phase 3: Onboarding (Week 4)

- 30-minute video onboarding call (batch of 20 ambassadors)
- Share onboarding kit (see below)
- Add to ambassador WhatsApp group
- Assign first month's activities

## 7. Content Guidelines

### What to Say

✅ "Bandhan uses AI to match you based on values, not just photos"
✅ "All profiles are DigiLocker verified — no fakes"
✅ "It's free to start — 5 profiles per day"
✅ "Your parents can see a Family View PDF of your match"
✅ "It's built for marriage-minded people, not casual dating"
✅ "Privacy-first — your data stays in India"

### What NOT to Say

❌ "This app guarantees you'll find a partner"
❌ "Better than Shaadi/Bumble/Tinder" (don't badmouth competitors)
❌ "You need to pay to use it" (emphasise free tier first)
❌ Any claims about success rates that aren't verified
❌ Anything religious, casteist, or discriminatory
❌ "My referral code gives YOU a discount" (there's no discount — be honest about rewards)

### Social Media Posting Rules

1. **Always use #BandhanAI and #CampusAmbassador**
2. **Never share others' profiles** without their explicit consent
3. **Use official templates** or get approval for custom content
4. **Disclose**: Add "Ambassador" or "🤝" to posts (transparency)
5. **No fake testimonials** — only share genuine experiences
6. **Respond to DMs politely** — you represent Bandhan

## 9. Dos and Don'ts

### DOs ✅

1. **Be genuine** — Use the app yourself first. Share real experiences.
2. **Focus on friends** — Personal recommendations > mass marketing.
3. **Listen to feedback** — Report what users say, good or bad.
4. **Respect "no"** — If someone isn't interested, move on.
5. **Be creative** — Your unique ideas are valued. Propose new activities.
6. **Stay active** — Consistent small efforts > one big push.
7. **Ask for help** — Reach out in the ambassador WhatsApp group.

### DON'Ts ❌

1. **Don't spam** — Never send unsolicited bulk messages.
2. **Don't create fake accounts** — Instant termination.
3. **Don't make false promises** — "You'll find a wife in 1 week!"
4. **Don't share personal data** — Never screenshot others' profiles.
5. **Don't harass** — If someone blocks you, respect it.
6. **Don't compare religions/castes** — Bandhan serves everyone.
7. **Don't use Bandhan branding** for non-Bandhan purposes.

### Termination Grounds

- Creating fake profiles or encouraging others to
- Harassment or discrimination
- Misusing Bandhan branding
- Sharing user data without consent
- Inactivity for 2+ consecutive months without notice

## 10. FAQ for Ambassadors

**Q: Do I need to be on Bandhan myself?**
A: Yes. We expect ambassadors to have an active profile. You can't recommend something you don't use.

**Q: Can I be an ambassador if I'm already in a relationship?**
A: Absolutely! You don't need to be looking for a match. Many ambassadors promote Bandhan for friends and siblings.

**Q: How much time does this take?**
A: About 2-3 hours per month for minimum activities. Most ambassadors spend 4-5 hours including bonus activities.

**Q: Can I be an ambassador at multiple colleges?**
A: No, one college per ambassador. Focus on your campus community.

**Q: What if nobody signs up through my code?**
A: That's okay in the first month! Focus on awareness first. Signups follow naturally. Reach out to your mentor if you need help with strategy.

**Q: Can I customise the promotional materials?**
A: Yes, but get approval first. Send your design to the ambassador WhatsApp group for review before posting.

**Q: When do I get my physical kit?**
A: Within 2 weeks of onboarding, shipped to your college/home address.

**Q: Can I refer another ambassador?**
A: Yes! If they get accepted, you earn 25 bonus points.

**Q: What happens after I graduate?**
A: You can transition to an "Alumni Ambassador" role — same program but focused on your workplace and social network instead of campus.
