## Email Guidelines

- **Sender:** Bandhan AI <hello@bandhan.ai>
- **Reply-to:** support@bandhan.ai
- **Frequency:** Max 2 emails/week per user
- **Unsubscribe:** One-click, immediate effect
- **Personalization:** First name, match names, metrics
- **Language:** English default, Hindi option in settings
- **Testing:** A/B test subject lines for open rate
- **Compliance:** CAN-SPAM + Indian IT Rules
