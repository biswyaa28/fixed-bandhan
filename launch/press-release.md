## Bandhan AI Launches India's First AI-Powered Marriage Matchmaking App with Government-Verified Profiles

*New app combines artificial intelligence, DigiLocker identity verification, and family involvement tools to reinvent Indian marriage matchmaking*

**Mumbai, India — [Launch Date] 2026**

Bandhan AI today announced the public launch of its marriage-focused matchmaking platform, the first Indian dating app to integrate DigiLocker identity verification and AI-powered compatibility scoring designed specifically for the country's marriage-seeking population.

### The Problem

India's online dating market is projected to reach $1.2 billion by 2027, yet 73% of users abandon dating apps due to fake profiles, and 78% of Indian users on these platforms are actually seeking marriage — not casual relationships. Legacy matrimonial websites feel outdated for younger users, while Western dating apps don't account for Indian cultural values around family involvement and marriage intent.

### The Solution

Bandhan AI addresses this gap with a purpose-built platform featuring:

- **AI Compatibility Scoring:** A proprietary algorithm that matches users on intent alignment (35%), shared values (25%), lifestyle compatibility (20%), location proximity (12%), and family fit (8%) — not just photos.

- **Three-Tier DigiLocker Verification:** Bronze (phone), Silver (Aadhaar), and Gold (photo + government ID) verification levels eliminate fake profiles entirely.

- **Family View PDF:** A shareable, print-friendly profile summary designed for parents — bridging modern digital dating with traditional family approval processes.

- **Safety-First Design:** Features include a "Share My Date" emergency button, optional "women message first" mode, 15-second voice notes, and in-app video calling that never requires sharing personal phone numbers.

- **DPDP Act 2023 Compliance:** All user data is stored exclusively in India with full compliance to the Digital Personal Data Protection Act. Users can export or delete their data at any time.

### Pricing

Bandhan AI offers a generous free tier (5 profiles/day, 2 conversations/day, full safety features) alongside Premium (₹499/month with 7-day free trial) and a unique Family Plan (₹799/month for 2 sibling profiles with a parent dashboard).
