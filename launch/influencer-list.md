## Rules

- **No paid influencer deals in Year 1** — organic only
- **Always offer genuine value** (free Premium, beta access)
- **Never fake testimonials or reviews**
- **Respect "no" — don't follow up more than twice**
- **Track all outreach in a spreadsheet** (name, date, status, result)
- **Prioritize relevance over follower count** — 5K engaged followers > 100K inactive
