### 4.3 Required Information
When filing a grievance, please include:
- Your **registered phone number** (for account identification)
- **Nature of grievance** (content, privacy, safety, payment, technical, other)
- **Detailed description** of the issue
- **Date and time** when the issue occurred
- **Evidence** (screenshots, message IDs, transaction IDs) if available

### 4.4 Acknowledgement
Every grievance will be acknowledged with:
- A **unique ticket number** for tracking
- Confirmation of the **category** assigned
- **Expected resolution timeline** based on the category
- **Contact information** for the assigned officer

## 5. Grievance Categories & SLAs

### Response and Resolution Timelines

| Category | Description | Acknowledgement | Resolution Target |
|----------|-------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| **Content Takedown** | Illegal or prohibited content reported | Within **24 hours** | Action within **36 hours** (IT Rules 2021 compliance) |
| **Privacy / Data** | Data access, correction, or deletion request | Within **24 hours** | Within **30 days** (DPDP Act 2023 §11-12) |
| **Safety / Harassment** | Threats, stalking, blackmail, abuse | Within **4 hours** | Within **24 hours** (immediate action for threats) |
| **Fraud / Scam** | Financial fraud, romance scam, impersonation | Within **24 hours** | Within **72 hours** |
| **Payment / Billing** | Refund disputes, billing errors, double charges | Within **24 hours** | Within **7 business days** |
| **Account Issues** | Login problems, suspension appeals, deletion requests | Within **24 hours** | Within **7 business days** |
| **Technical** | App bugs, crashes, performance issues | Within **48 hours** | Within **15 business days** |
| **General** | Other concerns not covered above | Within **48 hours** | Within **15 business days** |
| **Government / Law Enforcement** | Court orders, government directions | Within **24 hours** | Within **72 hours** |

### Service Level Agreement (SLA) Notes:
- "Acknowledgement" means written confirmation that the grievance has been received and assigned
- "Resolution" means a substantive response — either resolving the issue or explaining the outcome with reasons
- Complex matters may require up to 30 days with interim updates every 7 days
- Matters involving law enforcement or court orders are handled as per the specified timelines in the order

- **Contact:** Written communication to the registered office (postal only for this level)
- **SLA:** 30 business days
- **Scope:** Final internal review of unresolved grievances

## 7. External Remedies

If you are unsatisfied after exhausting the internal grievance mechanism, you have the following rights:

### 7.1 Data Protection Board of India
*(Once constituted under the DPDP Act 2023)*
- File a complaint under Section 13(2) of the DPDP Act 2023
- The Board may be reached at the address and website notified by the Government of India

### 7.2 Consumer Forum
- **National Consumer Helpline:** 1800-11-4000 (toll-free)
- **Online portal:** https://consumerhelpline.gov.in
- **District Consumer Disputes Redressal Forum:** For claims up to ₹1,00,00,000 (₹1 crore)
- **State Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission:** For claims between ₹1 crore and ₹10 crore
- **National Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission:** For claims above ₹10 crore

### 7.3 Cyber Crime
For complaints related to cyber crimes (hacking, identity theft, financial fraud):
- **National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal:** https://cybercrime.gov.in
- **Helpline:** 1930
- **Local Cyber Crime Police Station**

### 7.4 Women's Safety
For complaints related to harassment, stalking, or threats:
- **National Women's Helpline:** 181
- **National Commission for Women:** http://ncw.nic.in
- **Emergency:** 112

### 7.5 Civil Courts
You may approach the appropriate civil court of competent jurisdiction for any dispute not covered by the above remedies.
