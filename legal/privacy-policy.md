## 2. Definitions

| Term | Meaning (per DPDP Act 2023) |
|------|------------------------------|
| **Personal Data** | Any data about an individual who is identifiable by or in relation to such data |

## 7. Data Storage & Security

### 7.1 Storage Location
All data is stored on **Google Cloud Platform (GCP) servers located in India** (Mumbai region: asia-south1), via Firebase services. Analytics data is stored on our self-hosted Umami instance in India (Railway Mumbai or Indian VPS).

### 7.2 Security Measures
We implement security measures compliant with IT (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures) Rules, 2011, and aligned with ISO 27001 standards:

| Measure | Implementation |
|---------|----------------|
| **Encryption in transit** | TLS 1.3 (HTTPS) for all communications |
| **Encryption at rest** | AES-256 encryption on Firebase/GCP |
| **Access control** | Firebase Authentication, Firestore security rules, role-based access |
| **Input sanitisation** | XSS prevention, HTML stripping, Zod schema validation on all inputs |
| **Rate limiting** | 100 requests/minute per IP for APIs, 10/minute for auth endpoints |
| **Content Security Policy** | Strict CSP with per-request nonce |
| **PII redaction** | Automated PII scrubbing from all logs, error reports, and analytics |
| **Bot detection** | User-agent filtering, suspicious pattern blocking |
| **CSRF protection** | Origin/Referer validation on all mutating requests |
| **Brute-force protection** | Account lockout after 5 failed OTP attempts (15-minute cooldown) |
| **Security headers** | HSTS, X-Frame-Options: DENY, X-Content-Type-Options: nosniff |
| **Regular audits** | Weekly `npm audit`, monthly OWASP ZAP scan, quarterly manual review |

### 7.3 Breach Response
In the event of a data breach affecting personal data:
1. We will notify the **Data Protection Board of India** (once constituted) within **72 hours**
2. We will notify affected Data Principals **without unreasonable delay**
3. Notification will include: nature of breach, data affected, remedial steps taken, contact for enquiries

| **Location data (Share My Date)** | **24 hours** after session ends | Safety only, auto-deleted |

When data is due for deletion:
1. Automated cron job identifies expired data
2. Data is soft-deleted (marked for removal)
3. After 7-day verification period, data is permanently and irreversibly deleted
4. Deletion is logged for audit purposes (log entry contains no personal data)

## 10. Your Rights Under DPDP Act 2023

As a Data Principal, you have the following rights:

### 10.1 Right to Access (§11)
You have the right to obtain:
- A summary of your personal data being processed
- The identities of all Data Processors with whom your data has been shared
- Any other information as may be prescribed

**How to exercise:** Settings → Privacy → Download My Data (or email dpo@bandhan.ai)

### 10.2 Right to Correction (§12)
You have the right to correct inaccurate or misleading personal data, and to complete incomplete data.

**How to exercise:** Settings → Edit Profile (or email dpo@bandhan.ai)

### 10.3 Right to Erasure (§12)
You have the right to have your personal data erased when it is no longer necessary for the purpose for which it was collected.

**How to exercise:** Settings → Account → Delete Account (or email dpo@bandhan.ai with subject "Erase My Data")

**Process:**
1. Request submitted → Acknowledgement within 24 hours
2. 30-day cooling-off period (in case of accidental request)
3. After 30 days, all data permanently and irreversibly deleted
4. Confirmation email sent upon completion
5. Certain data may be retained if required by law (Section 8 retention periods apply)

### 10.4 Right to Grievance Redressal (§13)
You have the right to have your grievances addressed by our Grievance Officer and, if unsatisfied, to escalate to the Data Protection Board of India.

**How to exercise:** Email grievance@bandhan.ai (see Section 18)

### 10.5 Right to Nominate (§14)
You have the right to nominate another individual to exercise your rights in the event of your death or incapacity.

**How to exercise:** Email dpo@bandhan.ai with subject "Nominee Registration" and provide:
- Your name and account phone number
- Nominee's name and contact details
- Relationship to nominee
- A signed declaration (physical or digital)

### 10.6 Response Timelines

| Right | Acknowledgement | Resolution |
|-------|----------------|------------|
| Access | 24 hours | 30 days |
| Correction | 24 hours | 7 days |
| Erasure | 24 hours | 30 days (+ cooling-off) |

## 14. Analytics (Self-Hosted, Privacy-First)

### 14.1 Umami (Self-Hosted)
We use **Umami**, an open-source, self-hosted analytics tool. Unlike Google Analytics:
- Data is stored on **our own servers in India** (Railway Mumbai / Indian VPS)
- **No cookies** are used — Umami uses a hash of anonymised data for unique visitor identification
- **IP addresses are anonymised** (last octet stripped before storage)
- **No data** is shared with any third party
- Data is **auto-deleted after 90 days**

### 14.2 What We Track (Anonymised)
- Page views (which pages are visited, not by whom)
- Feature usage frequency (e.g., how often voice notes are used)
- Error rates (how often the app crashes)
- Performance metrics (load times, by network type)

### 14.3 What We Do NOT Track
- ❌ Individual user behaviour or journeys
- ❌ Personal identifiers (name, phone, email)
- ❌ Chat message content
- ❌ Profile viewing patterns (who viewed whom)
- ❌ Match or rejection decisions
