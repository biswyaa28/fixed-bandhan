## 3. Refund Eligibility

### 3.1 Full Refund (100%)

You are eligible for a **full refund** if **ALL** of the following conditions are met:

- Refund is requested within **48 hours** of the first purchase
- You have **NOT** used any Premium-exclusive features during this period
- This is your **first** refund request on the Platform

Premium-exclusive features include: viewing more than 5 profiles in a day, sending a Special Interest beyond the free daily limit, using advanced filters, or sending a Premium Interest.

### 3.2 Pro-rated Refund

You are eligible for a **pro-rated refund** (based on unused days) if:

- Refund is requested within **7 days** of purchase
- You have used some Premium features during this period
- Calculation: (Remaining days in billing cycle ÷ Total days) × Amount paid

### 3.3 No Refund

No refund is available if:

- More than **7 days** have passed since the purchase
- The account has been **suspended or banned** for Terms of Service violations
- The request is a **repeat refund** (second or subsequent refund request)

## 5. How to Request a Refund

### Option 1: In-App (Recommended)

1. Go to **Settings → Premium → Subscription Details**
2. Tap **"Request Refund"**
3. Select your reason from the dropdown
4. Add any additional comments (optional)
5. Tap **"Submit Refund Request"**
6. You will receive a confirmation email with a reference number

### Option 2: Email

Send an email to **billing@bandhan.ai** with:

- **Subject:** "Refund Request — [Your Registered Phone Number]"
- **Body:**
  - Registered phone number
  - Transaction ID or date of purchase
  - Reason for refund
  - Preferred refund method (if different from original)

### Option 3: Grievance Officer

If your refund request is denied and you believe it should be approved, contact:

- **Email:** grievance@bandhan.ai
- **Subject:** "Refund Grievance — [Phone Number]"

## 9. Exceptional Circumstances

We may grant refunds outside the standard policy in the following cases:

### 9.1 Technical Issues

If a technical issue on our end prevents you from accessing Premium features for more than **24 consecutive hours**, you are entitled to:

- Extension of subscription by the duration of the outage, OR
- Pro-rated refund for the affected period

### 9.2 Duplicate Charges

If you are charged more than once for the same subscription period:

- **Full refund** of all duplicate charges
- Automatic detection and refund within 48 hours (where possible)

### 9.3 Unauthorised Transactions

If an unauthorised person made a purchase on your account:

- **Full refund** upon verification
- We may request additional information to investigate
- We recommend changing your account credentials immediately

### 9.4 Bereavement / Medical Emergency

In cases of death or serious medical emergency of the subscriber:

- **Full pro-rated refund** upon submission of relevant documentation
- Contact billing@bandhan.ai with subject "Compassionate Refund"

### 9.5 Service Discontinuation

If we discontinue the Service or Premium features:

- **Full pro-rated refund** for the remaining subscription period
- At least **30 days' advance notice**

## 10. Non-Refundable Items

The following are **not eligible for refund** under any circumstances:

- ❌ Free trial periods (no charge was made)

- ❌ Third or subsequent refund requests from the same account
- ❌ Subscriptions purchased through promotional offers at ₹0 or 100% discount
- ❌ Any period after 7 days from purchase date (except exceptional circumstances)
