## 1. Be Honest

### ✅ DO:
- Use your **real name** (or a commonly used name)
- Upload **recent photos** (within the last 2 years) of yourself
- Accurately state your **age, education, occupation, and marital status**
- Be transparent about your **intent** (marriage, serious relationship, etc.)
- Disclose relevant information that a potential life partner should know

### ❌ DON'T:
- Create **fake profiles** or impersonate others
- Use **someone else's photos** (catfishing)
- Exaggerate or lie about your **income, education, or family status**
- Hide significant life facts (e.g., existing marriage, children)
- Use AI-generated or heavily filtered photos that misrepresent your appearance

---

## 2. Be Respectful

### ✅ DO:
- Treat every person with **dignity**, regardless of their background
- Respect someone's decision to **not respond or to unmatch** — it's their right
- Appreciate cultural, religious, and linguistic **diversity**
- Express preferences respectfully (e.g., "I prefer vegetarian" not "Non-veg people are bad")
- Use the icebreaker suggestions if you're unsure how to start a conversation

### ❌ DON'T:
- Use **casteist, sexist, racist, or communal** language
- Body-shame, age-shame, or income-shame anyone
- Send **unsolicited romantic or sexual** messages
- Pressure someone into sharing personal details (phone number, address, social media)
- Mock someone's religion, beliefs, language, accent, or appearance
- Make **dowry demands** or tie financial conditions to a relationship
- Send **repeated messages** after being ignored (this is harassment)

---

## 3. Be Safe

### ✅ DO:
- Use the **"Share My Date"** feature when meeting someone in person
- Meet in **public places** for the first several meetings
- Tell a **trusted friend or family member** about your meetings
- Trust your instincts — if something feels wrong, it probably is
- **Report** suspicious profiles immediately
- Use the **safety button** if you feel unsafe

### ❌ DON'T:
- Share your **home address, workplace address, or daily routine** with strangers
- Send **money** to anyone you've met on the platform, for any reason
- Share **bank details, UPI ID, or OTP codes** with other users
- Meet in **private or isolated locations** for early dates
- Ignore **red flags** (pressure to move off-platform, financial requests, inconsistent stories)

## 4. Prohibited Content

The following content is **strictly forbidden** and will result in immediate removal and potential account ban:

### 4.1 Illegal Content
- Content promoting or facilitating any crime under Indian law
- Drug-related content (sale, purchase, or glorification)
- Content related to human trafficking or exploitation
- Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) — reported to NCMEC and Indian authorities immediately

### 4.2 Hate & Discrimination
- Hate speech against any religion, caste, tribe, gender, or community
- Content promoting communal hatred or violence
- Casteist slurs or untouchability references
- Sexist content that demeans any gender

### 4.3 Explicit & Violent Content
- Nudity or sexually explicit content
- Graphic violence, gore, or content depicting injuries
- Content glorifying self-harm or suicide
- Content promoting eating disorders

### 4.4 Harassment
- Threats of physical harm or violence
- Blackmail or extortion attempts
- Doxxing (sharing someone's private information)
- Revenge sharing of intimate content
- Persistent unwanted contact after being blocked

### 4.5 Misinformation
- Fake news or deliberately misleading information
- Medical misinformation
- Financial scam promotions
- Conspiracy theories that incite hatred or panic

### 4.6 Commercial Activity
- Advertisements for products or services
- Recruitment for businesses or MLMs
- Solicitation for external apps or platforms
- Promotion of gambling or betting services

## 5. Profile Photo Rules

### ✅ Acceptable Photos:
- Clear, well-lit photos of **your face** (at least one showing your face clearly)
- Full-body photos where you are clearly identifiable
- Photos with friends/family (but tag who you are)
- Photos showing your hobbies, travel, or interests
- Professional photos or casual everyday photos

### ❌ Unacceptable Photos:
- Photos of **other people** without their visible consent (e.g., photos of celebrities)
- **Group photos** where you're not identifiable
- Heavily **filtered or edited** photos that misrepresent your appearance
- Photos showing **nudity or revealing clothing** that is sexually suggestive
- Photos with **weapons, alcohol, drugs, or smoking** prominently featured
- Photos with **offensive gestures, symbols, or text** overlaid
- Screenshots, memes, or text-only images
- Photos that are **blurry, dark, or unrecognisable**
- Photos of **children alone** (children may appear in family photos with a parent)

### Photo Limit: Maximum 5 photos per profile.

## 6. Chat & Voice Note Rules

### Messages:
- Maximum 1,000 characters per message
- No sharing of **phone numbers, social media handles, or external links** until both parties are comfortable
- No **copy-paste spam messages** sent to multiple users
- No messages in languages the recipient hasn't indicated they understand (default to English/Hindi)

### Voice Notes:
- Maximum **15 seconds** per voice note
- Must be in a language the recipient can understand
- No **abusive language, threats, or sexually explicit audio**
- No **background recording** of other people without consent
- No promotional or commercial content

### Conversation Etiquette:
- Start with a respectful introduction or use an icebreaker prompt
- Don't demand immediate responses — everyone has different schedules
- If you're not interested, a polite decline is better than ghosting
- Use the **Unmatch** feature cleanly — no hostile parting messages

## 7. Meeting In Person

### Before Meeting:
- Chat on the platform for at least a few days before suggesting a meeting
- Video call first if both parties are comfortable (in-app feature)
- Share meeting plans with a **trusted contact** using "Share My Date"

### During Meeting:
- Meet in a **public place** (café, restaurant, park, mall)
- Arrange your **own transportation** (don't accept rides from first dates)
- Keep your phone charged and accessible
- **Don't consume food or drinks** left unattended
- Trust your instincts — leave if you feel uncomfortable

### After Meeting:
- Use the **Success Tracker** feature to provide private feedback
- Report any concerning behaviour to safety@bandhan.ai
- **Don't feel obligated** to continue communication if it didn't feel right

## 8. Reporting & Moderation

### How to Report:
1. **In-profile:** Three-dot menu → Report Profile
2. **In-chat:** Long-press message → Report Message
3. **Safety button:** Red shield icon → Report concern
4. **Email:** safety@bandhan.ai with details

### Report Categories:
| Category | Examples |
|----------|---------|
| **Fake profile** | Stolen photos, impersonation, wrong gender/age |
| **Harassment** | Threats, stalking, persistent unwanted contact |
| **Spam** | Commercial messages, scam links, MLM promotion |
| **Inappropriate content** | Explicit photos, violence, hate speech |
| **Underage user** | Appears to be under 18 years old |
| **Scam/Fraud** | Financial requests, romance scams, blackmail |
| **Other** | Any behaviour violating these guidelines |

### Moderation Process:
1. Report received → Auto-acknowledgement within 1 hour
2. Automated screening for obvious violations (< 1 hour)
3. Human moderator review within **36 hours** (IT Rules 2021 compliance)
4. Decision: Dismiss, Warn, Restrict, Suspend, or Ban
5. Reporter notified of action taken (without revealing specific details)
6. Reported user notified of violation and action taken

### Transparency:
- We publish quarterly moderation transparency reports
- We cooperate with law enforcement for criminal matters
- We report CSAM to authorities immediately upon detection

## 9. Enforcement Actions

Actions are graduated based on severity and frequency:

| Level | Action | Trigger |
|-------|--------|---------|
| **Warning** | In-app warning notification | First minor violation |
| **Content Removal** | Offending content removed | Content policy violation |
| **Temporary Restriction** | Limited features for 24-72 hours | Repeated minor violations |
| **Temporary Suspension** | Account suspended for 7-30 days | Serious violation or 3+ warnings |
| **Permanent Ban** | Account permanently deleted | Severe violation, illegal activity, or 2+ suspensions |
| **Legal Action** | Referral to law enforcement | Criminal activity, CSAM, serious threats |

### Immediate Ban (Zero Tolerance):
The following result in **immediate permanent ban** without prior warning:
- Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)
- Threats of physical violence or murder
- Sharing intimate images without consent (revenge sharing)
- Confirmed identity fraud (catfishing with harmful intent)
- Solicitation of prostitution or escort services
- Drug trafficking or financial fraud

## 10. Appeals Process

If you believe an enforcement action was taken in error:

1. **Email:** appeals@bandhan.ai within **7 days** of the action
2. **Subject line:** "Appeal: [Your Account Phone Number]"
3. **Include:**
   - Your registered phone number
   - A description of what happened
   - Why you believe the action was incorrect
   - Any supporting evidence
4. **Review:** Appeals are reviewed by a senior moderator (different from the original reviewer) within **7 business days**
5. **Decision:** Final decision communicated via email. The appeal decision is final.
